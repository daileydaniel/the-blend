The Concept

Two Distinct Grapes,
One Great Bottle.

Walla Walla isn't just a pretty backdrop — it's one of the most celebrated wine regions in the country, known for bold Bordeaux blends. These blends are made by combining different grapes varietals, each with their own character, into something better together. That's not just winemaking. It's a partnership.

Over the weekend, you won't just visit wineries — you'll experience them. You'll spend the weekend with a small group of couples, take part in a guided winery hop with built-in prompts, and create two custom-blended bottles together.

It's fun, a little different, and something you'll actually remember — including a few conversations about your relationship you probably haven't had in a while.

How It Started

My wife and I have been together since we were teenagers — through big moves, career changes, and the chaos of life. We've had our ups and downs, but we've always chosen each other.

A few years ago, we started carving out intentional weekends away — time to reset and be intentional with each other. These trips became something we looked forward to — a chance to have fun, slow down together, and take an honest look at what was working in our relationship and what could use some attention. The Blend grew directly out of that.

About Me

I've spent over a decade in customer-facing roles in tech — bringing people together, reading the room, and knowing when to guide a conversation and when to let it happen. I'm also a certified yoga instructor, which taught me presence, intention, and how to slow down just enough to notice what's really going on. That mix is what shapes The Blend: structured but never rigid, thoughtful but never forced.

I created The Blend to give other couples a version of what those weekends gave us: time that's actually fun, intentional without being heavy, and genuinely different from the usual retreat.

FAQ

Everything You Need to Know
Before Applying.

How much do retreats cost?

Most retreats fall between $3,000–$3,500 per couple, depending on the location and accommodations.

What's included in the price?

Everything for the weekend — meals, the stay, and the experience. You just show up.

Where will we be staying?

Each retreat is hosted in a spacious, privately rented home — carefully selected to give the group plenty of room to spread out and unwind. Think hot tubs, fire pits, and fully equipped kitchens. Amenities vary by retreat, but the standard is consistently high.

How does booking work?

You'll apply through our application form, which lists the available retreat options. Once accepted, you'll receive full details and next steps to secure your spot.

What is the cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable, but will be applied to a future retreat if you cancel with at least one month's notice.

What if we want to come with another couple?

Great! If you come as a group of four, both couples receive a 10% discount. Just indicate this in the application.

Do you plan private retreats?

Yes — reach out by email and we'll talk through what's possible. Private retreats are shaped around what you're looking for.
